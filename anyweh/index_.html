About Anyweh.com

Anyweh.com was started in March 2008 by some great minds Phillip Glenister, Philip Mckenley and Damion Johnson.

Who for the love of the party decided to put this great website together with the idea to sharing the Jamaican party experience with the world.

With dedication and hard work we the anyweh team will be evolving continuosly from impossibility to possibilities.

We see the website as the direct connect between the patrons & the party, between the party & its promoters, its promoters and the party's popularity and the wider world.
